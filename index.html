Bomberman

Press ESC to quit this game

  • Player One Score: 0
  • Player Two Score: 0

Game Over

Press 1 for a New Game - Normal Level

Press 2 for a New Game - Speed Level

Your Goal is to defeat the other player.

How To Play

Player One:

Press → to Move to the Right

Press ← to Move to the Left

Press ↑ to Move to the Up

Press ↓ to Move to the Down

Press 'ALT' to Drop a Bomb

Player Two:

Press 'D' to Move to the Right

Press 'A' to Move to the Left

Press 'W' to Move to the Up

Press 'S' to Move to the Down

Press 'CAPS' to Drop a Bomb
